How to Make Pizza

Ingridients:


  • Tomato sauce

  • Cheese

  • Pizza Crust

  • Cup Bowl

Instructions

    1. Pour 1/2 cup of tomato sauce into the cup bowl
    2. Pour the Tomato Sauce to the pizza bread crust
    3. Take shredded cheese into the tomato sauce
    4. Heat up the oven for 30 minutes in microwave oven
    5. Let it cool for five minutes
